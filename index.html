RULES: TYPE, TAP OR CLICK TO SPELL WORDS 2 - 8 LETTERS LONG. EACH LETTER HAS A POINT VALUE; WHEN YOU SPELL A WORD THOSE POINTS ARE ADDED TOGETHER. THAT 'WORD SCORE' IS MULTIPLIED BY A MULTIPLIER BASED ON THE LENGTH OF THE WORD SPELLED. 2, 3 & 4 LETTER WORDS GET A 1X BONUS, 5 & 6 LETTER WORDS GET A 2X BONUS, 7 LETTERS GET 3X, 8 LETTERS A 4X BONUS. INCORRECTLY SPELLED WORDS ARE RESET WITH NO POINT DEDUCTION. THE 'DELETE' BUTTON ON YOUR KEYBOARD RESETS WORDS JUST LIKE THE RESET BUTTON ON THE SCREEN. THE ENTER BUTTON ON YOUR KEYBOARD SUBMITS THE WORD JUST LIKE THE SUBMIT BUTTON ON SCREEN. IF YOU CANT USE ALL YOUR WORDS YOU'LL HAVE TO START A NEW GAME. Contact me with any bugs you find.
